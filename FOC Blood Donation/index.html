CALL-based platform to connect blood searchers with donors

GiftOfLife is a real-time free platform to help blood searchers connect voluntary blood donors around Bangladesh.

What is GiftOfLife?

GiftOfLife is an automated blood service that connects blood searchers with voluntary donors in a moment through CALL. GiftOfLife is always a free service for all.

Why is GiftOfLife?

  • 100% Automated
  • Always free
  • 24x7 service
  • All data is secured
